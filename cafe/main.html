About Crumbs & Co

Welcome to Crumbs & Co, where we celebrate the sweetest things in life - cupcakes! Our cozy little cafe is a haven for cupcake enthusiasts, a place where every bite is a little piece of happiness.



Our Story

At Crumbs & Co, our story is as sweet as our cupcakes. It all started with a passionate baker and a dream. The founder, Sarah, fell in love with baking at an early age. Her grandma's secret cupcake recipes became the inspiration for what would later become Crumbs & Co. She dreamed of creating a space where people could gather and indulge in the simple joys of life, one cupcake at a time.


After years of experimenting with flavors, perfecting recipes, and sharing her creations with friends and family, Sarah decided to open Crumbs & Co. In 2010, our cozy little cafe opened its doors, and we've been spreading the love of cupcakes ever since.



Our Mission

Our mission is to make the world a sweeter place, one cupcake at a time. We believe that there's no problem in life that a cupcake can't make a little better. That's why we pour our hearts into every batch of cupcakes we bake.



Our Cupcakes

At Crumbs & Co, we take cupcakes to a whole new level. Our cupcakes are not just desserts; they're edible works of art. We use the finest ingredients, from locally sourced organic eggs and butter to premium chocolate and fresh fruits. Our skilled bakers create cupcakes that are not only delicious but also beautiful to look at. From classic flavors like Vanilla and Chocolate to unique creations like Valrhona Blonde Ganache and Toasted Marshmallow, our cupcakes are a feast for the eyes and the taste buds.



Our Cafe

Step into our charming cafe, and you'll feel like you've entered a cupcake wonderland. The aroma of freshly baked cupcakes fills the air, and our cozy atmosphere welcomes you to stay awhile. Whether you're catching up with a friend, savoring a quiet moment, or celebrating a special occasion, Crumbs & Co is the perfect place to do it.



Community and Sustainability

We are committed to giving back to our community and the environment. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible and aim to reduce our environmental footprint. We also support various local charities and organizations, making the world a sweeter place for everyone.



Join Us

We invite you to join us at Crumbs & Co and experience the joy of cupcakes. Whether you're a cupcake connoisseur or just looking for a sweet escape, we have a cupcake with your name on it. We can't wait to share our passion for baking with you and make your day a little sweeter.


Thank you for being a part of our sweet journey. We look forward to serving you and making your day a little brighter with our delectable cupcakes.


With love and sweetness,


The Crumbs & Co Team
